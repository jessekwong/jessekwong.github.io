2D Slasher

A 2D project I created to learn and test out the functions in Unity 2D.

In this project, I learned and implemented many elements of Unity 2D, such as animating a gameobject and its children, managing sprites and its interactions with masks and layers, hit detection and collision, and more

The player can change their weapons to gain different elemental buffs, which in turn can apply debuffs to enemies hit, such as burning, shocking and freezing
